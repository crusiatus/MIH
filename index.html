About Make It Happen Photography

We specialize in capturing your most precious moments, particularly graduation photography that celebrates your academic achievements.

Our Mission

To provide quality prints and frames that preserve your graduation memories for a lifetime. We believe every graduate deserves beautiful, professional portraits that capture their achievement.

What We Provide

Complete graduation packages including professional hair & makeup services, sanitized toga and barong/alampay, and assistance during your shoot. All garments are available in sizes Small to Extra Large.

Our Philosophy

"You can be with someone you love" - We believe graduation photography should capture not just individual achievement, but the support and love of family and friends who made it possible.

Why Choose Make It Happen?

  • Professional hair & makeup services included
  • Complete and legitimate graduation medals
  • Sanitized toga and traditional Filipino attire
  • Premium glass-to-glass frames for prints

Our Services Include

  • Unlimited shots during your session
  • Professional assistance during shoots
  • Family and couple portrait options
  • 1-month Google Drive access for all photos
